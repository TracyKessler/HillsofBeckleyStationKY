Events

Annual Yard Sale

Typically on a Saturday morning in June the neighborhood hosts its annual yard sale. It's a great way to clear out items from spring cleaning or to pick up some good finds around the neighborhood.

Summer Cookout

August 6, 2016 was our first neighborhood cookout. It was a great night to share food and quality time with our neighbors!

Dates of events are set just prior to the event. Check the neighborhood Facebook page for more details.
